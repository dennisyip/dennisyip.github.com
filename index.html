Dennis Yip

Summary

Dennis Yip is a designer and aspiring software engineer

I was born on Christmas morning in the year 1988, in Boston, Massachusetts. I grew up in Newton, a suburb approximately seven miles west of downtown Boston. I was raised in a traditional Chinese family and learned to speak Cantonese fluently as my native tongue.

At a early age I was fortunate to discover my passion for art and design.I was talented in free hand drawing, painting, and model building. In high school, I enrolled in graphic design and art classes to continue to improve my creative skills.

I attended the University of Massachusetts Amherst, graduating with a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Architecture and a minor in Chinese Language with academic honors. In college, I concentrated my studies in green building and sustainability. During my junior year, I studied aboard in Copenhagen, Denmark where I was able to experience studying design in a foreign culture.

After graduation I worked as a laboratory animal technician at Tufts University School of Medicine for two years. My job included duties as both a technician and supervisor to a facility housing up to forty thousand animals for biomedical research. While working at Tufts, I learned many valuable lessons about team work and individual leadership.

I am currently enrolled in Boston Startup Institute's Front End Ramp Up program. I hope to make a career change to become a web developer and utilize my graphic design skills.
