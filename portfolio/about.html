I was born on Christmas morning, in Boston, Massachusetts. I grew up in Newton, a suburb approximately seven miles west Boston. I was raised in a traditional Chinese family and learned to speak Cantonese fluently as my native tongue.

At a early age I was fortunate to discover my passion for art and design. I was talented in free hand drawing, painting, and model building. In high school, I enrolled in graphic design and art classes to improve my creative skills.
