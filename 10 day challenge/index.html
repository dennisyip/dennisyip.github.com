Dennis Yip

Summary

Dennis Yip is a designer and aspiring software engineer

I was born on Christmas morning in the year 1988, in Boston, Massachusetts. I grew up in Newton, a suburb approximately seven miles west of downtown Boston. I was raised in a traditional Chinese family and learned to speak Cantonese fluently as my native tongue.

At a early age I was fortunate to discover my passion for art and design. I was talented in free hand drawing, painting, and model building. In high school, I enrolled in graphic design and art classes to improve my creative skills.

I attended the University of Massachusetts Amherst, graduating with a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Architecture and a minor in Chinese Language with academic honors. During college, I had the opportunity to travel around the world and experience many new cultures that changed my life. I concentrated my studies in green building and sustainabilty. During my junior year, I studied abroad in Copenhagen, Denmark, one of the world's leading cities for green growth. While I was there I learned about the solutions around tackling challenges in building consumption of waste, energy and heating. Studying abroad opened my mind and pushed me to effetively communicate my ideas. Through college I developed strong skills in Adobe Photoshop, Indesign, and Illustrator from designing presentational boards for projects.

After graduation I worked as a laboratory animal technician at Tufts University School of Medicine for two years. My job included duties as both a technician and supervisor to a facility housing up to forty thousand animals for biomedical research. While working at Tufts, I learned many valuable lessons about communication and team work.

I currently applied to Boston Startup Institute's Front End Ramp Up program. In the program, I hope to develop my skills building web pages, learning proper spacing, interaction design, and visual design. Eventually, I hope to make a career change and find a job at a local startup where I can utilize my design skills.

When I am not in front my computer, I spend most of my time outdoors. I am a passionate rock climber. I spent weekends with friends finding new climbing spots and challenging boulder routes. Currently, I have also developed a interest in long distance running. This summer I have been running between 15-20 miles a week. I hope to run a half marathon within the next year.
